Project goals

Demonstrate a clean, accessible blog layout that uses semantic regions and proper headings, ready for styling or framework integration.

Semantic checklist

  • Use one <main> per page and keep it unique.
  • Wrap each post in <article> with its own header/footer.
  • Split content with <section> and descriptive headings.
  • Place complementary info in <aside>; don’t mix with main narrative.
  • Mark menus with <nav> and add aria-label where helpful.
  • Pair media with <figure>/<figcaption> and good alt text.
