Building a Semantic Blog Article

By Dev Student · September 29, 2025

Why semantics?

Semantic tags make content accessible to assistive technologies, improve SEO, and create a clear document outline for maintainable CSS and JS.

Core layout regions

  • <main> holds the primary content of the page.
  • <article> wraps the self-contained post that can stand alone.
  • <section> divides the article into themed parts with headings.
  • <aside> contains complementary info like a TOC or author bio.
  • <nav> provides site or in-page navigation links.
